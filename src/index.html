At DCI everyone is welcome

Since our foundation in 2016 we have a mission: We want to help people find a secure job in Germany. It is not important to us what your background is, your skin color or who you love. We only care about the way you think: be open minded, be brave and ready for a change. To take your future into your own hands and think technology anew. For that we offer many different courses such as Web Development, Online Marketing, Programming and Human Resources. There is something for everyone! So what are you waiting for: Take a look at our courses and start your new path.

  • 100+ courses have started since we were founded
  • 110+ different nationalities of students and teachers
  • 600+ hiring partners
  • locations all over Germany
  • 80% placement rate

A job in the digital industry is boring and static?
Nothing could be further from the truth. It is colorful, different and even creative. People from all over the world come together to think of new solutions and test them everyday.
So the digital industry is always changing - with new positions, challenges and more freedom than in other branches. Companies that want to remain competitive have to join and change themselves as well. For that they need experts - and this is your chance.
